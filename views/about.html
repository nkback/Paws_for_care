About Us

Our Mission


At Paws to Care it is our absolute mission and motivation to heal your animals. We know how similar to a family member your pets can become and we've taken it on ourseleves to treat every patient with that same paradigm. We've been treating patients for over two decades and we have enjoyed serving families every day.

Meet the Vets


Viktor Ivanov is one of our longest working veternarians that has worked here. His special attention to helping animals has helped families for over two decades. His expertise on small animals has provided a place for many families concerned about their little pets where they've had no where else to turn.



Jen Bowers is our youngest vet who has brought excitement to the team with a proven speciality in treating dogs. Her ambition and compassion towards helping animals is unparalleled. And although she is young, she's as skilled as anyone in our office.
